It's me again!


This is an upgrade to my glider 1.0 mod. Now, the glider constructs itself from two elements. Pause the game, and put a single pixel of GliderBack and a single pixel of GliderFront next to each other (not diagonally). Let the game run, and the glider will assemble itself and start moving.
