Goal

Drag the green Thing to the yellow gate (End).

[edit]

Elements

Here are the descriptions of the elements in this mod.

[edit]

Gates

Gate (red) - This gate will kill the Thing upon contact. Go through the blue Switch to turn it off.

RigGate (purple) - You can drag the Thing safely through this gate, but beware! It is rigged!

FlameGate (light brown) - Beware of the flames that come out of this Gate. Can be turned off by the blue Switch.

End (yellow) - Touch this to win the game.

[edit]

Harmful objects

Bomb (dark brown) - A bomb connected to a burning Fuse...

Rocks (grey) - These are often triggered by a RigGate.

[edit]

Other

Switch (blue) - Drag the thing across this to turn off gates

Wire (light grey) - Used to connect Switch with the gates

Thing (green) - Drag this to the End.
