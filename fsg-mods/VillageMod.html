Village mod v. 1.86

Created by Jabberwock33

In the village mod, you can create people who will create different types of villages, cities, and fortresses across a landscape created by you. You also have many tools at your disposal that will affect how they develop and interact.

New in 1.86: -Bug fixes in farms, religion, scorpions, and dwarves.

New in 1.85 -Protoplasm is much more interactive, and so are dwarves.

New in 1.81 -Just a bug fix, gave + or - 1 or 2 to the rgb values of each element so that sandboxes would work. Some had previously been the same.

New in 1.8 -Reworked dwarves, which are now much more fun to fool around with. -Made scorpions vulnerable to disease and death.

New in 1.7 -Changed disease -Added terraforming tools -Changed scorpions -Changed elves

New in 1.6: -Elves -Dwarves -Scorpions -Altered religion and insanity -Dragons

New in 1.5: -Lava -Nukes -Radiation -Unholy energy to balance the holy energy

New in 1.2: -Villagers and builders chop away forests more quickly -Villages automatically deploy soldiers when attacked

New in 1.1: -Protoplasmic blobs (credit to armageddis) -Fences for restricting villager, citadel, protoplasm, and bandit growth
