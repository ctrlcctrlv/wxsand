This is my "eChemMod" - A chemistry mod with a lot of interactions between elements and compounds. I tried to make it has comprehensive as possible. I didn't make a "recipe book" so if you can't figure out how to make a compound you will need to use (wikipedia, where I got all my information from. I based this mod off the original default elements, so any similarities in code are unintended. I'm focusing on the interactions more than the explosions, and when I feel that I am done with the interactions, I will probably put in explosion and pexplosion lines. I really need coldself to get Water to turn into Ice and Melted Glass to turn into Glass. Thanks to da_evillittleman for inspiring me to make this chemistry mod after I downloaded his and realized that his was missing a lot of stuff(mainly Hydrochloric Acid). Also, thanks to Master_Hector for making his great mods, unique elements, and giving me the idea for explode3 and maybe explode/explode2 also, but those were an attempt to be like explode3(I like the result so I kept them in). The "e" in "eChemMod" may stand for Eric(my name), evil, extreme, enormous, explosive, etc.

Note - I haven't added silicon or any metal elements yet because A. I'm not totally sure if I'm finished with the current elements. B. Adding silicon, which is as reactive as carbon, would result in more compounds to create and research. C. I would need to make a lot of lines for the acids burning though the metals (such as rust). D. I would rather attempt to make interesting explosions.

Note - A few of the interactions are chemically incorrect, either because I didn't change them after I added the neccessary compounds, or I just didn't have the neccessary element(most likely Calcium). I will eventually try to make the existing compounds more exciting and maybe add new ones as I find them and if I still have room, add another element and the related compounds.
