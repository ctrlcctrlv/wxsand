[edit]

It's Finished!!

Cool! It's a soda fountain!

Load this picture into wxSand. The six water activated switches on the right-hand side of the fountain dispense stuff. The top two dispense water, the middle two dispense sand, and the bottom two dispense oil. After you've used the switches up, you can add the elements manually. There's a self-destruct switch inside the fountain, under all the normal switches. And there's a switch in the bottom right of the fountain that lets liquids out of the tray. Of course, you can always remove the plastic cup. This uses my new teleportation system.
