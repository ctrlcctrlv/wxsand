This is my set of mods, complete physics file. My mods start off with some modifications to Spout and Torch, followed by various sources for powdered elements and degenerative sources. Then, there is Acid, which eats through most solid objects. Lava will slowly destroy wall, and will for no particular reason evetually destroy itself as well. It sets fire to stuff and melts sand and salt. The self-destructive process is sped up by the presence of water. Barrier is immune to all but Plant, and Alkali is like ??? except it only consumes Acid and Empty. Condenser condenses Steam and also freezes Lava. Awesome! should be placed behind ??? in the Leftover???. Blackhole absorbs everything. Leftover???, Remains, and MoltenCera are visible in the Misc group due to handling errors.
