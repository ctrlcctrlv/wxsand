ManaMod 1.0

Lots of new elements! I've sorted them into semi-meaningful groups. Lichen and Moss, are basically the clasic plant and my own take on it, respectivly. Wax is Cera, I tried to make it act more like in the original game, with moderate success. To be fair, I should point out that some of these elements weren't my idea, but all of them have been modified in some way, possibly quite extensivly.

Besides new elements to play with, there are two puzzles. SampleA and SampleB in the WierdStuff group.

SampleA is one of the other elements with a temporary dye added. See if you can figure out which one it is. (No fair just reading this file.)

SampleB is something much stranger. This unknown substance is one of the ingredients in the amazing CloningFluid (Special group) which can copy any liquid or powder. See if you can make SampleB into CloningFluid without using any of the elements in the Special group. It won't be easy.

Note, however that this mod was made with version 4-20060302 and hasn't been updated yet. So a few things may not work correctly in the current version. (Gate adn Key, for example.)
