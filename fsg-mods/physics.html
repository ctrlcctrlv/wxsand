Preface

"The Lord... Bringer of life and mayhen, delight and pain; The nothing, the begin and the end... keeping the key to the freedom of the soul."

"A concepts immersive Mod"


To begin action, turn on "boundary walls", and then just click stop button and release one Lord. But keep watching as The Lord can vanquish himself.


Alternately you can deploy a radius 4 Fruit, or Plague, or one of the Cheeses, or Eden Water, or Plague, Neuron, Cyborg...


There are serveral effects to you to discover at Lord's branches (read at game for more descriptions). Here are some: Vulcan HellPit SandTempest CryingFly LevitatingGollem IceHeart EdenLivingWater Plague MayhemCheese HavocCheese BlackHole...
