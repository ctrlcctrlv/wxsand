Kurisu Sand's Space Station Mod Guide v1.0

Probably the last version, everything will be covered in this guide. If you can't build a fairly good station after reading this, then you can ask me, SP57, or SP2 for help. Gamefreak12321 Is the current developer of this Mod and he has done an excellent job on it. Any elements that get repeated in a certain category on the mod will not be redone in this mod, element will only appear once on this guide.

[edit]

Misc

BombDeployer: Draw a line of this and connect it to power core, it will then drop a row of blue bombs, at the moment it only destroys containment wall. It only distorts other things.

[edit]

Stellar Objects

AsteroidBelt: Draw a line of this to make a row of Asteroids, Loose asteroid, and Worm can form out of this. Worm can destroy your station as well as Loose Asteroid.

Planet: I prefer to draw a 128 pixel sized circle of this, but you can draw it any way you want. Immediately after being drawn Alien pod ships start spawning out of it. These Alien ships can latch onto your spacering and send tracers through your spacering. If they touch OxygenCrewQuarters, they will create an Alien spawn point which spawns aliens and kills your crew.

Sun: Again you can draw a circle of this or any other shape you like, Sometimes used for just looks (eg. Engines) this can be deadly to your station, It spawns Solar Flare and Solar energy from it, Solar Flare can destroy your station, But Solar energy can be harnessed to supply power to your station using Solar energy collector.

Eclipse: At the moment only used for looks. It creats a Lunar Eclipse type of formation. (I use it for other things *hint* *hint*)

[edit]

Space Station

Power Core: This is used to spread power throughout your station. You can draw very small sized line for complexity or you can just draw larger lines for a higher rate of success that the power will spread all the way around.

SpaceRing: Use this to draw the outline of your station. Can also be used to fill space in your station. Basically its an all around element.

Solar Collector: Used to collect Solar energy, Draw a line of this, or a U shape if you like, and then connect it to power core. Then drop some solar energy on top of it. Sweet Sweet power >:D

Shield Generator: Use this to outline your station. Used as a safety precaution. connect it to power core and it will generate a bright blue shield outline. As long as the explosion isnt too deep, it will regenerate the shield. but if its too deep it wont.

[edit]

Life

Astronaut Bay: After you have made your Crew Quarters, draw a line from inside the crew quarters to outside your station, It will then convert Crew into Astronaut and it will Transport them outside your ship. If you put WeaponsLocker inside your crew quarters and make armed crew, armed crew will then be transformed into Armed Astronaut. Armed Astronaut can destroy planet if they hit it.

Crew: Basically this is your main life form, You first need to make Oxygen crew quarters, then you fill it with this. This can be used for MANY things, add crew to genetics lab in order to make it work, add crew to robotics bay in order to make it work.

Armed crew: when aliens attach themselves onto your ship and start spawning aliens inside your crew quarters, armed crew can kill some of the aliens.

Friendly Alien: This is kind of like crew, but they do nothing..I generally dont use Freindly alien at all. Mostly because they are useless.

Crew Quarters: Draw this inside of Space Ring, then draw a small spot of Oxygen creator inside of it and supply it with power, It will then create Oxygen Crew Quarters. You can put any form of life in here.

Genetics Lab: This can make "plantlife" projects inside your ship. First you add crew to it, then supply it with power, then add a Project of your choice, then add seed. In order to make a project grow, you need watermist, what you need to do isnt too complicated, first draw a row of dots of oxygen creator and sprinkler. Then draw a line of power core straight across the top of that. Like this:

O=oxygencreator S=Sprinkler P=powercore G=GeneticsLab

PPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPP OOSSOOSSOOSSOOSSOOSSOOSSOOSSOOSSOOSS




GGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGG

After you have made something similar to that, connect that line of power core to some power and it will start dropping watermist. Then you may add the project of your choice, and then the seed.

Instant Oxygen Crew Quarters: This is useful if you want to quickly create some oxygen crew quarters. The easy way to do this is to use the size of your choice and draw a dot and hold the mouse click down for 2 seconds. the other way to do it would be to left click-then right click right after that.

Oxygen Creator: Once you have made Crew Quarters, put a dot of this inside of it and supply it with power, it will then creat Oxygen crew quarters which will allow you to put life inside of it.

[edit]

Projects

Project 1: If you add this to genetics lab, it will allow you to grow a plant and it will be immune to the effects of vacuum.

Project 2: If you add this to genetics lab, it will allow for you to grow a seed into a plant that can live inside of vacuum. (empty) The vacuum (empty) will act as a water just like for normal plants in the default mod. It will let the plant grow at a fast rate.

Project 3: THIS ONE! Will allow for you to grow a plant that will eat your spacestation and turn it into a plant, so basically..your station turns into a plant. It still works..its just a plant. I will put a Biostation guide at the bottom of this guide.

Sprinkler: see Genetics lab to see what this does.

Weapons Locker: Put a dot, or line of this inside of oxygen crew quarters, and it will turn all of your crew into armed crew. See Astronaut bay and Crew to see what armed crew can be useful for.

Transport

Transport Tube In: Put this at one end of Transport Tube, this is the "loading Pod" your crew will go inside of this and flow through the transport tube.

Transport Tube Out: Put this at the opposite end of Transport Tube in, after the flow of crew is transported to this end Transport tube out will allow for the crew to come out of the transport tube.

Transport Tube: Use this to transport crew from one crew quarters to another crew quarters.

Construction

Robotics Bay: Draw a line of this touching vacuum (empty), and supply it with power, then add crew to it, and it will start creating Robots, which will build Scaffolding, Engine Scaffolding, and Weapons Scaffolding.

Scaffolding: This is used to draw "smaller ships" draw an outline with this, and lets robots continue building it and it will become ship.When its finished it is a dark blue/green color.

EngineScaffolding: This is used as an engine along with Scaffolding and Weapon Scaffolding. This can be finished by robots, it will become Engine when its finished. It is a light blue color when its finished.

Weapons Scaffolding: This is used as a weapons area for you "ship". Robots can finish building this, and when its finished it will become Weapon. It is white when its finished. ( no it does not shoot anything).

Engineering Station: This will create robots which make Space Ring. Basically they expand your station. I personally dont use this, but its your station, go ahead if you want to.

Destruction

Black Hole: In the newest version this dosnt suck anything up, but in earlier versions it used to create compressed space and it "sucked" things up. This can also be used as a power supplier. Draw a dot of blackhole and draw a circle of containment wall around it, then use probe and draw a line touching blackhole to outside of the containment wall. Then connect the probe to power core. It will supply quick pulses of energy.

Destabilizer: When you draw a line of this it creates small explosions on that line you drew. If you draw it inside of space ring, it will create Critical Station. which will eventually blow up. It will sometimes also create Boom.

Fire: OK fire, well I knew what It did, I had just forgotten, but when you build biostation, biostation will create something called Shell (see biostation guide at the bottom). fire will burn shell as it is closely realated to a type of wood. I'm gonna be a good guy and put in here that Gando346 reminded me what fire does. Thanks Gando346

Ghost Missile: Ok barely anyone knows how to use this, First when you have drawn space ring, and if you happen to draw planet right next to your space ring, the planet will spawn alien ships, those ships will attach themselves to your space ring, and create Tracers inside of it. all you have to do it draw some ghost missile inside of space ring, and it will destroy the tracers, the explosion, instead of fire, turns into more space ring.

Mini Explode: This is basically just fire, but if you draw a line of this through any power core, you can say bye bye to your station, it will completely destroy it.

Particle ???: This is basically the ??? element in the default mod, But this will destroy only particle elements in this mod.

Firer: This is used as a gun. the only way to explain how to set this up would to be give you a diagram..so here it is:

F=firer LI=liquifier C=containment wall

LILI C C C C C CCCCC CFF CCCCCCCC

its sort of like an "L" shape that you have to make, once you do that supply the liquifier with power and it will drop liquid energy, which in return is what gets fired in the end.

Time: This is a destructive force which is created when you put AirElementCreator, WaterElementCreator, FireElementCreator, and LightingElementCreator next to each other in a line and put a line of power core across the top of them. it will create time which eats through anything except containment wall and probe. Using the same methods from blackhole, you can also get energy from time, Draw a square of contaiment wall, and fill it with time, and put a line of probe at the bottom, and connect probe to power core, it will supply a steady rate of power burst.

BioStation Creation Guide

In order to make biostation, first you need to make a working geneteics lab, with watermist covering it, see genetics lab above ^ Category Life to see how to make a full genetics lab. After you have made your genetics lab, all you need to do is add project 3 to it, then add a line of seed touching the genetics lab and also touching spacering. It wont take long until you start to see results, dont worry its ok to let it eat your station, everything will still be working. Ok next I will list what elements are turned into once you have biostation.

Growth Spurt: This is made when CR touches biostation, so once you have made a good amount of biostation you just need to make some solar flare touching PhotoReceptor and allow it to travel through the PhotoReceptor far enough to touch. Basically this is just "power burst" but for biostation.

Crew Quarters: After you have made biostation, you may add more crew quarters if you like, immediately after you add the new crew quarters it will be oxygen crew quarters, the biostation will automatically give oxygen to crew quarters.

Photo Receptor: This is made when biostation eats solar collector, or solar energy. This "eats/absorbs" Solar Flare. When solar energy gets eaten it is kind of like plant, just think of solar energy as water and drop it on top of photo receptor. When it absorbs that solar flare it make what is called "CR" and when its filled up enough and it travels to touch biostation, it causes the Growthspurt.

CR: This is what photo receptor turns into as it is absorbing the solar flare, it allows for biostation to turn into growthspurt.

Shield Generator: When this gets eaten it turns into shell 1 and shell 2. This is virtually just another type of shield.

  • Shell can only be made after you have made Growth spurt.
