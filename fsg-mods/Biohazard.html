Description

Yet another "electricity" mod, it adds new elements like "Positivo" (positive pole), "Negativo" (negative pole), "Socket", "Foco" (lightbulb) and "Cable" (wire).

Positivo and Negativo properly connected (left), a Foco and a Socket (right)

It works like this, you need a Positivo and a Negativo, you can connect them togheter with cable (wire) or just make them contact.

Once this happens, they'll become active (once active, you can't pass a cable from one to the other or it is going to short-circuit and the wire will get hot and burn), now, to turn the Foco (lightbulb) on, you need to make it touch a socket, then pass two Cables(wires), one from an active positive pole to the socket, and one from an active negative pole, the lighbulb will then turn on (it may take some time if you made the wires too long).

If you want to make your elements interact with this electricity, I suggest you make them react to "socketact". You can use my elements if you don't change the names and credit me in your physics file if you release it.

[edit]

Electric Elements

All the elements properly connected and the names of each on screen
[edit]

Positivo

The positive pole of the battery, must be connected to a negative pole to become active

[edit]

Negativo

The complement to Positivo, both must connect to become active.

[edit]

Cable

The wire, it's used to conect the Positivo and Negativo, and to the socket. Once the electric poles become active, connecting them will cause a shortcircuit, the cable will get hot and burn.

The lightbulb has turned on
[edit]

Socket

The socket connects to an active Positivo and an active Negativo with two cables. Then it activates and can turn on the Foco (lightbulb).

A shortcircuit has been introduced
[edit]

Foco

The lighbulb, will turn on if connected to a properly configurated socket. It will get hot when turned on, so it can ignite oil or boil water. Because of the limitations of the game, it will not turn off if disconnected.

[edit]

Filter Elements

These six new elements allow only their specific element to pass through. Only water can pass through WaterFilter. There are filters for Water, Sand, Salt, Oil, Steam and SaltWater.

[edit]

Minor changes

- Fire: Now, a little bit of it goes down. Also, it turns to Smoke after a while (Smoke from ManaMod). I think it's more realistic.

- Foco: The lightbulb will now get hot while on.

- Electrolysis: An active socket or a wire connected to an active positive or negative pole can separate water into it's components. Socket generates both Oxygen and Hydrogen, a positive wire creates Hydrogen, a negative wire creates Oxygen.

- Added sources for all the liquid/gaseous elements, for convenience.

- Oil will now combust if exposed to high temperatures.
