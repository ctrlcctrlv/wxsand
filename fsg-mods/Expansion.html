Alchemy Mod

Want to make your own potions and test them on your very own subject? Now you can!
Creator: Luod0153 and Barefootpete and Bletotum
System: WxSand
Date of Release: May 10, 2007
Current Version: v0.7
Mod Type: Mixing
Forum Thread
Have fun!

[edit]

Recipe Book

Tips: It's always best to add water onto the ingredient, as most ingredients float on water, making mixing difficult. If a potion needs to be heated, it is advised to heat the mortar from below with a burner, and not a Heatplate, unless the recipe says otherwise. It is advised to wait a short while before feeding another potion to your subject

[edit]

Dulling Potion

Type: Remedy

Effect: Remedies the Color Potion, and the Dragon Skin Potion.

Preparation: Mix Mugwort and Ginger.

Facts: This is so dull, that it is even invisible.

[edit]

Color Potion

Type: Potion

Effect: Turns the Subject many different colors.

Preparation: Mix unfiltered Aloe_Vera with Sugar.

Facts: Easily remedied with the Dulling Potion.

[edit]

Dragon Skin Potion

Type: Potion

Effect: Gives the Subject dragon scales.

Preparation: Mix Redwort, and Firesalts.

Facts: Easily remedied with the Dulling Potion.

[edit]

Doom Potion

Type: Poison

Effect: Utterly destroys the subject, leaving a horrible mass of burning puss behind. Make a small portion of it, because it sends little meteors down of it, one at a time.

Preparation: Mix Nightshade, and Wasabi.

Facts: I have no idea how anyone could mistake this for anything else to ingest.

[edit]

Brain Hemoraging Potion

Type: Poison

Preparation: Use the new Scream-Knife to get the subject to scream, then mix that scream with some sunflower oil.

Effect: FATAL. The brain gets small tumors, then bursts into flames, and then the rest of it is burnt up too.
