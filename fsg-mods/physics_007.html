[edit]

Summary of Custom Elements

Pepper: Is like salt, in that it can combine with water to make a different kind of water, Pepperwater. Does nothing on it's own.


Pepperwater: Is formed from Pepper and Water. It was originally supposed to be oil formed from water, and as such it burns. It will also mix and eliminate water.


Ice: Is compacted Snow, and will melt when faced with Fire, Lave, or Salt.


Snow: Behaves like Ice, except can compact into Ice.


Blizzard: Self-explanatory. It makes Snow and Steam because it melts Ice that forms and blocks the flow of Snow.


Groundwater & Crudeoil: Are VERY dense versions of Oil and Water. Will react with Salt, Plant, Fire and Ember respectively.


Algae: Saltwater Plant.


Herbivore & Carnivore: (To be updated a lot) Can eat a few types of elements (including their own special element), and can return to basic elements when they come into contact. I'm still working on some improvements.


Food & Plantfood: The main diet of the two above elements.


Sun: Will turn any type of water into Steam, including Ice and Snow. Does not get put out like Fire, and doesn't effect anything else like Torch.


Recycler: Turns ANY element into Steam, except Salt, which burns through it. It includes two elements from coppro: Acid and Lava, so if you don't have those or different versions of them, it will error.


ETERNITY//cycle

Not your average element (at least to me), ETERNITY//cycle is the combination of two elements into what happens to never end.

How to start it:

First, contain everything within Wall. Then make a circle of Eternityfire in the center (so that the walls of the screen don't touch it). Wait a few seconds. It will deteriorate. Put any size of Eternityplant anywhere in the fire. It should start to grow into the empty spaces. If it doesn't survive more then 3 seconds, then you should wait 2 seconds and re-try it. When it makes a large bloom, you've done it correctly.

I find it interesting because it is eternal (35min of testing) and also looks cool. Somewhat like Game of Life with elements and sped up a lot.

Fire burns plant, but since it can grow fast enough into empty spaces, it splits and grows in blooms all around the circle. It always manages to survive. Plant seems to sprout from thin air in some cases.

Note: Some leftover elements are there. Disregard them. Also, there is a second version of ETERNITY//cycle that can be made by giving Eternitywater the ability to take over Eternityfire. I made this later, but the frame rate is 2 to 3 and it really stops looking cool after it slows down.
