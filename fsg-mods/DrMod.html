Walk Through and Pictures

A basic body for SpaceMonkeys' DrMod.

This section contains a Walk Through and a Picture for each procedure.

[edit]

Brain Tumor:

A man with a brain tumor

Steps:

  1. Set up an IV.
  2. Use scalpel to carve a nice notch out of the top of his head that touches the Skull (Note if you touch the brain with scalpel it will die)
  3. Use bandages and Blood_Cleaner to completely clean out all of the blood from the notch.
  4. Use a 1 pixel wide line that travels from just outside the brain to just inside the brain using Hole1 and Hole2
  5. Quickly use scalpel to isolate the tumor first, and then remove it. (Be careful again with the scalpel and the skull because if you get blood into the brain it will die)
  6. Use Brain_Fluid to fill in the empty area inside the brain, if it turns into a tumor than you didn't completely remove the tumor. Let it overflow outside of the normal brain area.
  7. Check to make sure you have no empty space inside the brain and then add Fake_Skull to finish the skull, again, make sure that you don't leave any empty space.
  8. Add skingen carefully to the notch that you cut out in the beginning to regenerate the body.


[edit]

Clogged Artery:

A man with a Clogged Artery.

Steps:

  1. Set up an IV.
  2. Set up a Heart-Lung Machine (Heart - Heart-Lung_In - Oxygenator - Heart-Lung_Out - Artery thats touching cerebellum)
  3. Cut an Incision separating the clog with the healthy artery.
  4. Place a bandage along the cuts.
  5. Draw bypass from the 2 separate arteries, connecting them.(Have the heart-lung machine and bypass on the same side of the artery)
  6. Wait for the Artery to turn green and then add Defrib to the heart.(Note: This may take up to a minute)
[edit]

Kidney Failure:

A man with Kidney Failure.

Steps:

  1. Set up IV.
  2. Separate the Kidney from the Artery using a scalpel.
  3. Use Kidney Remover on the Kidney.
  4. Stitch up the open wound.

Cataract:

A man with Cataracts.

Steps:

  1. Wait for Cataract to completely cover the eye.(Do not set up an IV)
  2. After that very carefully remove the orange blur from the eye.
  3. Finally set up your IV and let it cure your eye.

Gangrene:

A man with Gangrene

Steps:

  1. Set up an IV.
  2. Use scalpel to amputate off the body as close as possible to the gangrene, if the scalpel does not touch the gangrene you have failed as a doctor because you didn't do the best job possible (Scalpel must touch the gangrene)
  3. Quickly add bandage before the gangrene crosses over the cut.
  4. If the gangrene got through make another cut and add more bandage.

Cyborg Parts:

(No Image Needed)

Steps:

  1. Set up an IV.
  2. Add Cyborg-Body to the missing area (Body part that is missing)
  3. Make a size 8 (8 is not required but it is a good size to use) circle of Body-Battery that is touching both the heart and the body (It does not need to touch the actual Cyborg-Body).
  4. A wave will come and power your Cyborg-Body also destroying almost all death present. If all the death isn't destroyed than you need to let the IV do the job.

Robotics:

The Robotics was created as somewhat of a miniature side mod to the Dr.Mod. The Robots do not fuse well with cyborg body parts and therefore have a not so friendly reaction.

[edit]

Making Robots

(No Image Needed)

Steps:

  1. Create a Needle
  2. Add Nano-Bots to the Needle
  3. Inject Nano-Bots into the body.
[edit]

Viruses

(No Image Needed)

Steps:

  1. Place Virus outside of the robotic body.
  2. (Optional)Wait for the robotic body to become aware (or alert) that there are viruses inside of it.
  3. You must add Anti-Virus to cure the virus, the Nano-Bots inside of the body (element RW) will help fend them off, but they cannot destroy the virus.
[edit]

Appendicitis

(No Image Needed)

Steps:

  1. Either have death touch the appendix or add appendicitis directly to the appendix.
  2. Quickly remove the appendicitis from the body or else it will explode
  3. If it explodes it will cause gangrene, quickly rid the body of the Gangrene and then remove the pus and blood that exploded.
[edit]

Tape Worms

(No Image Needed)

Steps:

  1. Add "Bad Pork" to the mouth and let it travel around the body.
  2. Once you see tapeworms form quickly inject your person with Tape Shot and then remove the Tape Worms with the scalpel.
  3. Tape Worms often cause appendicitis, so you will need to follow those steps as well.
