My primary goals

The primary goal of this mod is to have as much detail as possible with its element interactions, as well as for it to have the same sense of discovery that the original Hell Of Sand had. It also gives me the chance to learn about the new functions in the latest versions of Wxsand, as well as let me get some new ideas over and done with rather than having them sit in my head.

I hope to never include an element that sombody else has done, if I do I'll be sure to make it in more detail.


[edit]

Interesting Elements

Here I'll explain a bit about the more interesting elements. (35 in total, 13 visible excluding spouts).

Lava heating Rock at a distance.
[edit]

Lava

In addition to radiating hot air and bubbling, Lava now cools into Rock.

[edit]

Rock

This one is interesting because of its interactions between Pebbles and itself. Its also strongly inter-twined with Lava. One of my biggest problems with the default file/game is that 'Wall' never has any interactions, and I never knew what material it's supposed to be. Now it has a few interactions. In addition when heated it will radiate heated air.

Water bubbling from the heat of Lava.
[edit]

Water

Water is cool, really cool. One of the more fun things to do with it is put some in an inclosed sphere and manually heat the bottom, once it starts bubbling/boiling remove the heat source. The bubbling stops almost immediately (like real life when you take a pot off of the burner).

[edit]

Plants

I've never been very fond of the plants in the sand games because they never really do much besides blindly take over everything that is water. In my mod, the plant isn't so cut and paste (I didn't modify it much, so it still kindof sucks). Plant grows from a variety of different fluids at a variety of different speeds (Depending on the fluid).

Lava melting rock.
[edit]

Instapipe

This one is my latest addition to the Mod, its fairly self-explainatory; it's a pipe that when drawn instantly fills itself with water. The pipe itself can be melted into Lava, the water would then start to fall out. Or you might make one and put a hole at one end to empty it, then add a Water Spout to the other and have some sort of tube. Instapipe only works with a pen size above 4, and above pensize 128 its a bit slow. however you can cross the pipes without leaks.

Instapipe in action.
[edit]

Closing

So experiment and try all sorts of combinations, I've tried my best to have as many possible interactions between the elements present as possible. I may update it as new Ideas come to me. Feel free to contact me on the Falling Sand forums if you liked my mod, or if you have any suggestions.

Also, special thanks to Zaukul for the bubbling effect that I gleamed from his physics file (With his permission). :)
