Kingmoo's Cool Plant Mod

Basically, you put down some earth, stick some seeds in it, and give a little sprinkling of water, it should grow upwards and form lots of pretty flowers. Fertiliser helps roots to grow, and if a root reaches Empty, and you add Water, then it will grow into a new plant.

Enjoy.
