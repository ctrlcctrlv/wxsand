Pacman Mod!

How to play: 1.Make a large block of wall. 2.Fill it with tunnels of empty arranged into a maze. 3.Put pellets and power pellets in the maze. 4.Put one pacman and one of each ghost (Blinky, Pinky, Inky and Clyde) in the maze. 5.Play a game of FSG pacman! 



#wxSand: Owen Piette's Falling Sand Game
#Version 4.3, File subversion 2
#Pacman Mod!!!!!!
