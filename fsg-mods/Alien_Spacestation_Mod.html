GUIDE

Elements and descriptions on how to use them.

Here are some sandboxes for your personal use and recreation needs.

The All new ship..The Sheath

[edit]

STELLAROBJECTS

Empty - I need not explain.

Earth - Earth generator, gives off little human space shuttles that latch onto your ship and send in humans into your ship to kill of your Alien race.

Sun - Sun, give off solar flares and solar energy which can be cropped to power your ship.


[edit]

SPACESTATION

PlasmaTube - Used to transfer energy, Sends a plasma burst through these tubes to energize your ever so needed weapons and what not.

AlienVessel - This is your base of your ship, Draw this and you would make the frame of a ship.

SolarCollector - Used to collect solar energy and then turn it into energy, connect it to PlasmaTube.

ForceField - Use this to create a shielding around your ship to protect you form certain deaths. Connect it to power to charge this and create your Forcefield.

SAlienBay - Allows aliens to leave their respective ships, Armed aliens, when colliding with earth, will destroy it.

ContainmentWall - An indestructable wall that can be used for anything. Only weakness is heat, which only makes it change color.


[edit]

LIFE

Alien - Your basic lifeform used in your ship, can be armed and released into space, or kept inside to defend a human invasion.

OxygenCreator - Connect this to power and to AlienQuarters to give your Aliens a living Enviroment.

WeaponsLocker - Put this inside the AlienQuarters to allow your aliens access to weapons, when needed.

AlienQuarters - Your aliens main Living Quarters, Oxygenate it to allow life to flourish inside it.

Cryo-Stasis - Build these and fill them with life, they will contain "frozen" aliens which can later be brought back to life, just connect power to the cryo-stasis tubes.

Biolab - Use this to contain the Experimental_Gas. This biolab room is made out of a material that is not affected by the gas.

[edit]

TRANSPORT

TeleportIn - Aliens get inside this to teleport them to another part of the ship.

Teleport - The Empty space that Aliens teleport through, connect TeleportIn and TeleportOut with this.

TeleportOut - The Exit point for Teleported Aliens.


[edit]

DESTRUCTION

BlackHole - Exactly as the name states, it's a blackhole that will destroy everything in its path..except that invincible containment wall.

Destabilizer - Destabilizes the atoms surrounding it to create explosions. Draw some inside or near AlienVessel and it will cause a critical station or Boom.

SelfDestruct - Destroys human Attacks on your ship, just drop some inside AlienVessel, and when Humans latch onto your ship the self destruct will destroy their ships.

MiniExplode - A Small explosion used to destroy or torture your poor Aliens.

Fire - Well...it's just fire.

Time - Very corrosive, it will melt through anything.

Deatomizer - Deatomizes your AlienVessel and ForceFields.

Comet - A comet, very hard to stop and usually breaks apart, also gives off an experimental gas that the aliens are currently working on. Be careful of the gas as it has highly unusual impacts on your ship life and your ship itself.

Experimental_Gas - Be careful as it has highly unusual impacts on your ship life and your ship itself.

[edit]

CHEATING

Plasma - The energy that travels through PlasmaTube.

SGW - The energy that travels through ForceField.

SolarEnergy - This is what is given off of the sun, use this to spawn random SolarEnergy.

SolarFlare - This is something given off of the sun also, This is very dangerous though and can destroy your station if it comes in contact with it.


[edit]

POWER

ParticalEnergy - This is the Energy created from the Partifier, A rather stable source of energy.

Partifier - This is a new Alien technology used to create their own energy, connect it to PlasmaTube.

Probe - connect this to BlackHole or time to Transfer the energy from those destructive forces, to be used in your ship.

MatterInducer - Place this in the center of a vacuum inside of a Reactor.

Reactor(off) - Use this as a casing for your vacuum.

Vacuum - Fill a Reactor with this.

North - Connect this to south to create a magnet for powering your station.

South - Connect this to north to create a magnet for powering your station.

ParticleEX - Place this in the center of your magnet, gives off particles that become charged with the magnet. Place a line of PlasmaTube inside so that your particles can touch it.

Chamber - Surround your magnet with this.

QC - Essentially a Quantum computer, give this power and connect it to Reactor(off) otherwise your reactor will never work.


[edit]

ATOMIZERS

Deatomizer - Deatomizes your ship.

Reatomizer - Used to Reatomize your lost ship particles due to Deatomizer.


[edit]

WEAPONS

Disorganizer - A strange discovery on the Alien's part, Used to drop on top of Ships to slowly eat away at them.

EMPBomb - Connect this to the bottom of your ship and supply it with power to drop forcefield destroying bombs.

Phaser - Sends an atom phaser that destroys anything that it touches.

Track - The guided firing system for the Phaser.

EnergyBeam - An advanced weapons developed as a prototype, make sure to surround this with BeamHousing as if you don't you might end up being destroyed yourself.

Crono - A Time disruptor. Can be mixed with time but only at your own risk.

GaiaCannon - Put some of this on the bottom of your ship to apply some serious damage to under flying ships. You only get one shot out of it and then it needs to be replaced. Watch out for the recoil. Make sure to connect to power.

Screamer - Uses highly concentrated sound wave to Shake the target apart, can cause minor explosions on rare occasions. Just draw a line of it on the bottom of your ship and connect it to power.

PhotonGun - Uses Magnetized Protons and electrons to fire them at the target, highly unstable as the trail that this gun leaves is constantly exploding.

Coagulator - When this hits the ship it causes a chemical reaction to occure leaving only Disorganizer.


[edit]

SAFETY

SafetyBomb - Place these around your ship to prevent destruction from Critical Station or Boom.

Bulkheaddoor - These doors are safety precautions for leaking quarters, just draw a line straight across a hallway to cut off the non-affected area from the leaking area. Draw a trigger in a spot around the wall and then connect it to the Bulkheaddoor with Safetywire.

SafetyTrigger - See Bulkheaddoor.

Safetywire - See Bulkheaddoor.

Cutoff - Used to protect your ship from the devastating recoil of the gaiacannon.

Back-Recoil - Another piece used to protect your ship from the recoil damage for the gaiacannon.

More will be added as the updates continue, any questions that need to be answered please ask them on the thread. I will post the question and the answer here and also in the thread's first post.


[edit]

ENGINE

Mass_Reflector - This is the engine of your ship, connect it to power and rig it up to your ship.

Disruptor - The Primary weapon for any ship, connect it with power and watch it fire. The Weapons group was full so it has to go here.

Light - This is just for looks, your can place the anywhere on your ship. They are selfpowered so now connection to plasma is needed.
