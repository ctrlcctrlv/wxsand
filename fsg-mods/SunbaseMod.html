Sunbase Mod

Build a base inside the sun and see how long it can last! Please turn sandbox temperature up to 98 while playing this mod. 


#subversion 2

group	Solar	Sun	Storm	Flare
group	Base	Air	Wall	WallII	Powercore	Wire	Cooler	Stormguard	Firecontrol	Stasis
group	Life	Crew
group	Fire	Fire	Ember	Meltdown

#The Sun. Crank up the sandbox temperature so that it gives off heat. The higher the heat, the tougher it will be to keep your base running.

#Open space.
