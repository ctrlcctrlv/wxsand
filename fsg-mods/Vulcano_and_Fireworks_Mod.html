This Mod features 2 types of lava of wich 1 simulating an eruption (sord of), 2 visible types of rock (of wich 1 melts), ashes, meltable crystals, a terraform element wich creates a landscape to play in, an overpowered coolant element ;) and lastly 8 diffirent firework explosions.

<<UPDATE!!>> 1.2 Some bugfixes.

<<UPDATE!!>> 1.3 Made some fixes to several elements to make playing a little easyer and balance out some things better. Also added several helper elements wich you may find interesting such as a Wall/WAsh remover :)

<<UPDATE!!>> 1.4 You may have noticed some element help was missing, I've corrected this.
