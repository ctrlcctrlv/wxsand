Updated version of the October Mod-of-the-Month!

Dave's Mod is mostly a pyro/destruction mod, but is also something like Lynx's Mod, a huge compilation of customized elements with numerous interactions between them all.

[Features of Dave's Mod]
-Awesome Demolition- blow stuff up or burn it down in a huge variety of unique ways. Create entire buildings out of concrete, then smash it down with a single stick of dynamite placed at the base for an awe inspiring collapse.

-Pyro's Dream- with elements such as napalm and firestorm and a complete set of burnables including wood, coal, and plants at your disposal, what other mod would you ever need?

-Truly Unique Plants- requiring soil, seed, and water, these grow into much more than the standard green blob. You won't find these in any other mod, I can promise you that.

-Nice Graphical Effects- most of the work put into this mod has gone into making everything look stunning. Little details like wood charring black while it burns and fire transitioning from bright orange to deep red help pull this off.

-Reworked Basics- everything in this mod has been rewritten from the ground up, even basic elements like Wood, Fire, Plant, Oil, Wax, and Water.

-Heating/Cooling- virtually all the elements are temperature consious, for instance, wood can be made to spontaneously combust simply by heating it, no fire necessary.

-Candles- with wax that actually behaves like wax as a pose to the "might as well be wood" wax many other mods try and pull on you.

-HUGE Explosions- with one of wxsand's most complicated explosives, taking up over 300 lines of code and 100 elements alone! This is definately not your run of the mill cheap "explosion dot" that seems to be so popular recently.

-So Much More- dilutable acid, humans, zombies, spouts, unique interactions, etc.

[The Complete NewB's Guide to wxSand]
A step by step guide for those who just don't get it.

Step 1: Download the latest wxSand:
This is the particle engine that runs the various mods available at these forums.

Download and install wxSand from here
http://fallingsandgame.com/viewtopic.php?t=2708&postdays=0&postorder=asc&start=0

Step 2: Get Ready
Open a new, blank document in the basic text editor of your choice. For PC users, just use notepad.

Step 3: Download Dave's Mod
Navigate your web browser here
http://www.fallingsandgame.com/wiki/index.php/User:AcidReign/physics

See that BIG light blue box of "code looking" text with the dotted line around it? Very carefully select all the text inside the box. It should be many, many pages of text, get it all. Be careful not to get anything extra, only the stuff inside the box should be selected.
Once you've highlighted it all, right click on it and select copy. Switch to notepad and click open the "Edit" menu at the top and select the "Paste" option.
Click open the "File" menu at the top and select the "Save As..." option.
Name the file "Dave'sMod.txt" and save it wherever you installed wxSand.

Step 4: Run Dave's Mod

Open wxSand's executable file and click open the "File" menu at the top and select the "Load physics.." option.
Select Dave'sMod.txt and click "Load".

Step 5: Enjoyment
Enjoy Dave's Mod!
