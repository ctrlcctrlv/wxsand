A mod about creating sweetlooking Crystal Structures. It was more of a test for the wxSandClassUtil (worked great :D), but nontheless its a nice mod ;)

You dont have to use the cheat elements, you can also get those elements from more complex reactions Geriat is easy Puronium is hard
