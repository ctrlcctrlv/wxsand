This is my fireworks mod for WxSand v4.4

it is currently version 1.1


[edit]

The HOW-TO:

Explosive: goes BOOM when lit.

Skywriter: draw a shape, then light on fire. It makes fiery shapes

The 5 rockets: They fly. For a few, draw in 1 or 2 dots or lines. for 1 full minute of rockets (and some eye pain) draw in larger blocks.


TO MAKE A FIREWORK:

1. make a straight line of rocketpath from the ground to the middle of the sky. (to make a straight line, draw with the right-click button)

2. put a blob of kaboom at the top of the rocketpath.

3. put a fuse touching the bottom of the rocketpath, and leading away from it.

4. pour a medium to large amount of X4 on top of the kaboom. (make sure it eats the rocketpath too)

5. light the fuse and watch!
