Description

This is based on c--b's mod, and adds some new elements and interactions. c--b's elements remain in their original categories, although I have modified some slightly (for instance, to remove splashing). My new elements are in the Darren's category.

A brief summary follows. The list of interactions is not exhaustive; play around with the elements to get a feel for what they do. I haven't listed the hidden elements.

  • Steel behaves a bit like c--b's Rock, except that it is a little more metallic. It melts into MoltenSteel, and rusts in Water.
  • SteelFilings are fragments of Steel. They too can rust and melt. When poured over Wood, they produce SawDust.
  • MoltenSteel behaves like c--b's Lava, except that when cooled it turns back into SteelFilings.
  • Rust is corroded Steel. It can be melted, and Pebbles can grind it into SteelFilings. It melts and burns in a different manner from Steel.
  • AbsoluteZero will unheat MoltenSteel, Steam and Lava back into SteelFilings, Water and Pebbles. It only has limited power to affect heat energy, however. The best way to use it is to run a thin stream of hot fluid over several lines of AbsoluteZero.
  • ColdFire works in a similar way to AbsoluteZero but otherwise behaves like Fire, and is less powerful.
  • Wood is my own implementation of wood. It can be infected by Fungus, and ground into SawDust using Pebbles or SteelFilings.
  • WetWood is wood that has been saturated by water. It doesn't burn as well, and gives off Steam when it does. It is easier to infect with Fungus, too.
  • SawDust can be ignited, and will very slowly decay in water.
  • Fungus can infect Wood, Plant or their variations. It gives off Spores, and burns more readily than uninfected material. Water and Mud will also affect it.
  • Spore is the seed of Fungus. If a Spore touches Wood or Plant, it will infect it. Spore interacts with various elements, and you can produce a large, complex fungal growth (a mushroom) easily by laying down a few Spores and pouring Mud over them.

Spouts are provided for SteelFilings, ColdFire, SawDust and Spore.
