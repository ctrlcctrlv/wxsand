This mod allows you the use of several species of worm like creatures called "Bytes." Some are weak against others, while some dominate others. Some have special abilites like Multiplication or fire resistance.

The bytes are:

Byte:The original byte that can morph into any other byte using crystals.

Pyrobyte: A byte that survives and multiplies in fire.

Aquabyte: A byte that dwells and multiplies in water.

Terrabyte: This byte can tunnel through Rock.

MitoByte: This byte can preform Bianary Fission.

Replibyte: This byte replicates through other bytes.

Zombyte: Can morph other bytes into zombies.

Combustibyte: Explodes on contact with other bytes.

Toxibyte: Can survive in poison.

Deibyte: The lord of bytes. It can only be killed the explosion from a combustibyte, or the byte destroyer. Otherwise, it is invincible. May also give seizures for those sensitive to light. You should see the hotcolor line I used for the tail. hotcolor DB 9999 999999 999999 It gives it a dark red, flashing color with blue and white towards the head.

Electrobyte: Releases little bolts of electricity when it is attacked.
